OUR ACTIVITIES

Here are some of the activities that we engage in:

Football Tournament

Sports bring people together and promote a healthy lifestyle. Our club hosts an annual football tournament that encourages friendly competition, teamwork, and sportsmanship among students. It's a great opportunity for participants to showcase their skills and enjoy the spirit of the game.

"Ask Lmjareb" Student Orientation Event

We understand the importance of a smooth transition for new students. To facilitate their integration into university life, we organize the "Ask Lmjareb" event, where experienced club members and university staff provide guidance, answer questions, and share valuable insights about campus resources, courses, and extracurricular activities

Squid Game

We organize exciting and engaging events inspired by the popular television series "Squid Game." These events promote teamwork, problem-solving, and friendly competition while fostering a sense of community among our members.
